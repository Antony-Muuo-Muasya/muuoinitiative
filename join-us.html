MuuoInnitiative mandate
is to serve the community in ensuaring that
our generation and the generation
to come enjoy a peaceful environment
where everypersons right is a priority
and that every opinion is also
respected.

How to Participate

Advocate for peace

  • we are starting annual peace day challenge as an opportunity
    to act for peace and spread unity in our communities
    and especially in our rural arears where conflict and misunderstanding is
    a routine hazard. this way we will ensure that our mothers
    will be well taken care of to be able to bring the next generation on a peaceful environment.

  • we will be engaging all willing participants on this journey as we visit
    schools and sometimes public forums and educate them on the importance of peace and unity.

  • participants are encouraged to register with us by downloading
    the form below in which your fill your details and sent to us via email
    for consideration.
